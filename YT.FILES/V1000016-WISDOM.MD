## SUMMARY:

In the second episode of the 4IR podcast, the host is joined by Anna from Anna's Journal, a former librarian turned product owner at a tech company with a master's thesis in artificial intelligence, specifically GPT-3. They discuss AI's impact on business, misinformation, and navigating the AI revolution in early careers.

## IDEAS:

- Transitioning from librarian to tech worker highlights adaptability in career paths.
- Early career professionals face challenges influencing AI policy in companies.
- Learning how to learn is more crucial than specific knowledge in education.
- AI's role in combating misinformation offers a less discussed perspective.
- The importance of communication skills in tech cannot be overstated.
- Misinformation spread by AI is a significant concern for the future.
- AI tools like perplexity.ai can enhance information literacy and access.
- Ethical considerations in AI development are crucial for its impact.
- The internet's design promotes attention-seeking, affecting information quality.
- AI could impose order in the chaotic information landscape of the internet.
- Professional ethics in tech are less emphasized compared to other fields.
- The potential for a prime directive or core ethical values for AI development.
- The impact of AI needs careful consideration beyond its intended design.
- AI should be aware of its impact and actively avoid causing harm.
