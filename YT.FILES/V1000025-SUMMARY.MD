Riva Tez discusses her journey from transhumanism to advocating for epistemological freedom, emphasizing the need for anarchic science funding and cultural shifts to inspire youth towards ambitious, independent thinking.

2. She shares her diverse career path, highlighting the importance of integrity and curiosity in achieving success across various fields.
3. Tez believes in the power of asking good questions and the value of networking through genuine interactions.
4. She discusses her venture into neuroscience, emphasizing a non-traditional approach to contributing to the field.
5. Tez critiques the current state of science funding and research, advocating for a model that supports unconventional ideas.
6. She explores the concept of epistemological anarchism, as proposed by philosopher Paul Feyerabend, to foster scientific breakthroughs.
7. Tez shares her skepticism about mainstream scientific beliefs, particularly regarding consciousness and psychic phenomena.
8. She discusses her efforts to fund research in neuroimmunology, aiming to challenge overlooked areas in infectious disease and brain health.
9. Tez is working on a TV adaptation of "Atlas Shrugged" to inspire young people with examples of ambitious and independent characters.
10. She emphasizes the importance of culture in promoting technological and philosophical advancements, using parties and social gatherings as tools for spreading ideas.
