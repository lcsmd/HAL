ONE SENTENCE SUMMARY:
A tutorial on installing and running private, customizable large language models on your computer using AMA software for data privacy.

MAIN POINTS:
1. Demonstrates installing a private large language model (LLM) on your computer for data privacy.
2. Highlights the ability to run models locally using your computer's processor and GPU.
3. Emphasizes that no external connections are made, keeping all data private.
4. Explains the availability of thousands of models online for various needs.
5. Introduces specialized models, including finance-focused and uncensored options.
6. Guides through downloading AMA software for model installation.
7. Provides a step-by-step process for downloading and installing models on Windows, Mac, or Linux.
8. Showcases how to find and install popular models like LLaMA 2 and uncensored versions.
9. Details how to interact with the installed model via command line for asking questions or coding.
10. Offers additional functionalities like saving sessions, exiting, and listing installed models.

TAKEAWAYS:
1. Installing a private LLM on your computer allows for secure, personalized AI interactions without external data sharing.
2. AMA software facilitates easy download and installation of a wide range of LLMs.
3. Users can choose models tailored to specific interests or needs, enhancing the utility of the AI.
4. The process is accessible for users across different operating systems, ensuring broad applicability.
5. This approach supports both privacy-conscious users and those seeking specialized AI capabilities.
