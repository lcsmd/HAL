# SUMMARY

Justin Murphy interviews Riva Tez about epistemological freedom, Feyerabend's influence on science, funding innovative research, and living a life of curiosity and integrity.

# IDEAS:

- Riva Tez believes success comes from integrity and curiosity, not just expertise.
- Tez argues for more epistemological anarchism in science to foster breakthroughs.
- She suggests that failure is a valuable data point for improving one's model of the world.
- Tez emphasizes the importance of asking good questions to learn from experts.
- She highlights the need for spaces and funding for scientific anarchy to save science.
- Tez discusses her transition from various careers, attributing her success to honesty.
- She criticizes the linear progression model in scientific research as limiting.
- Tez proposes that real scientific shifts come from refuting current paradigms.
- She advocates for funding projects that challenge conventional scientific methods.
- Tez shares her skepticism about the brain producing consciousness, favoring alternative models.
- She expresses interest in phenomena outside traditional scientific understanding.
- Tez discusses the potential of the mind to influence physical reality.
- She explores the idea of consciousness as an intention network connecting people.
- Tez is working on neuroimmunology research, focusing on overlooked areas of science.
- She plans to create a research foundation with a bounty model for funding specific studies.
- Tez aims to inspire the next generation by showcasing ambitious and successful individuals.
- She is adapting "Atlas Shrugged" into a TV series to present inspiring archetypes to youth.
- Tez believes in creating cultural movements to shift young people's perspectives on success.
- She supports decentralized systems like crypto to bypass traditional power structures.
- Tez emphasizes the role of culture in promoting new ideas and technologies.
- She advocates for creating fun and engaging experiences to attract people to innovative projects.

# INSIGHTS:

- Integrity and curiosity are foundational to navigating diverse careers successfully.
- Scientific breakthroughs often require challenging established paradigms with anarchistic thinking.
- Failure provides critical insights for refining one's approach and understanding of the world.
- The brain's role in consciousness may be more akin to an antenna than a producer of consciousness.
- Innovative funding models like bounties can direct research towards neglected scientific areas.
- Inspiring cultural narratives can motivate young people towards ambition and innovation.
- Decentralized technologies offer pathways to circumvent traditional institutional limitations.
- Engaging cultural experiences are essential for attracting interest in new ideas and movements.
