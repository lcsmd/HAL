SUMMARY:
This content narrates the life of Adam West, known for his role as Batman, covering his early life, career highs and lows, personal struggles, and legacy.

IDEAS:
- DC Comics initially portrayed Batman using a gun and killing villains, which changed due to societal concerns.
- Frederick Wortham's book in 1954 criticized comic books for juvenile delinquency, influencing Batman's non-lethal approach.
- The 1960s Batman TV show revived the character with a comedic angle, becoming a hit.
- Adam West faced typecasting and struggled with personal issues post-Batman, including depression and alcoholism.
- Despite early fame, West's career faced challenges due to being typecast as Batman.
- West's personal life was marked by multiple marriages and struggles with infidelity and alcohol.
- Adam West's early life on a farm contrasted sharply with his later celebrity status.
- His mother's regrets about her career sacrifices impacted West deeply, influencing his feelings about fame.
- West's expulsion from school highlighted his rebellious streak and foreshadowed future challenges.
- Despite early setbacks, West found success in Hollywood but struggled with the consequences of fame.
- West's portrayal of Batman became iconic, blending comedy, wholesomeness, and drama effectively.
- The Batman TV show's success led to West being typecast, limiting his career opportunities.
- West's personal indulgences mirrored the free-love ethos of the 1960s but had personal costs.
- Despite initial success, Batman's ratings declined, leading to the show's cancellation and West's career challenges.
- West's third marriage to Marcelle Tagand Lear provided stability during his career lows.
- Struggling for roles post-Batman, West took on various low-budget projects to stay afloat.
- West experienced a resurgence in popularity through voice-over work and appearances on talk shows.
- His role as Mayor Adam West in "Family Guy" introduced him to a new generation of fans.
- Adam West's death in 2017 was marked by Walla Walla shining the Bat-Signal in his honor.
